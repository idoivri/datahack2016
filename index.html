What is DataHack?

DataHack is the first data-driven hackathon in Israel, designed to bring together the Israeli data community. The hackathon is a 48-hours event where data enthusiatic programmers, designers and researchers combine their skills to build innovative solutions for real world problems.

Registration is open to all, but applications will be carefully reviewed for skills and experience. Participants will have complete freedom selecting data, tools, and project ideas.

Next

Information for participants

Participants will be chosen based on experience, skills and diversity criteria. Here are the basics:

Location

Jerusalem! An exact location will be announced soon.

Theme

Data hack is all about data. Every project is required to have processing and/or analysis of some interesting data at its core. It can be a machine learning algorithm, a statistical analysis or producing deep insights automatically with data mining techniques.

Date

The event will take place between Wednesday afternoon, September 21st and Friday afternoon, September 23rd, 2016.

Participation

Registration to the event is open to all - students, researches, designers and industry professionals.

Data

We will provide you with different data sets from our sponsors, partners and online sources, but you are free to bring your own data. Have a data-related question?
Contact our data team at data@datahack-il.com

Community

Besides a yearly hackathon, DataHack is also a vibrant community of data scientists and machine learning experts in which you can take part through our Facebook page, the DataTalks meetup series and our newsletter!
