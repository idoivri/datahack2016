Data sets

Here are some great open data sets we think you might like to work with but feel free to use any data set you like

Enron Emails

Enron was a U.S. energy-trading and utilities company that housed one of the biggest accounting frauds in history. Enron's executives employed accounting practices that falsely inflated the company's revenues, which, at the height of the scandal, made the firm become the seventh largest corporation in the United States. Once the fraud came to light, the company quickly unraveled and filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy on Dec. 2, 2001. The dataset contains ~200,000 email messages from ~150 users, mostly senior management of Enron.
